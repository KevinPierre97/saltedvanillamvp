About Us

Welcome to Salted Vanilla's Closed Beta v3

Hello friend !

Salted Vanilla is a scented candle cataloging web app. Our main goal is to help candle lovers keep track of their candle collection !

Users can add candles to the site, review existing candles, and create a few lists !

Since v2, we added have/had/want lists and improved our Search function. Please let us know what you think about our features !

I welcome thoughts about the app. Please send me your thoughts to SaltedVanillaApp @ gmail.com !

Thank you for inquiring Salted Vanilla !
